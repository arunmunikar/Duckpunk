nilah

Newari & Japanese Bhatti


Moving beyond fanaticism to a borderless approach to culture, DuckPunk Innovations is our culinary think tank & blank canvas where we allow ourselves to dream, explore and create.
Co-founded by two best friends, Akash Tandukar & Sujen Manandhar, our aim is to bring back the culinary legacy of 'Guru Cha', a family legacy that once reigned supreme in a tiny village nestled in the Himalayan Mountains of Nepal.
We explore the known, the unknown & the hidden, a team of culinary pirates & wanderers with a passion for travel.
